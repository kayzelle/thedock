About


Our Goal

The Entertainment Engineering & Design Program at the University of Nevada, Las Vegas finds Art Walk to be a great showcase of our interdisciplinary status between the College of Engineering and College of Fine Arts. The DOCK is a completely EED-student-run project consisting of multiple subprojects, including, but not limited to:

  • Audio
  • Lighting
  • LED
  • Content Creation
  • Graphic Design
  • Event Design
  • Schematics
  • Fabrications
  • Prop Making
  • Project Management
  • Hospitality
  • Marketing
  • Videography
  • Stagehand / Set-Up
  • Behind the Scenes
  • Web Development
