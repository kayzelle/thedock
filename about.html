About

The Entertainment Engineering Program at the University of Nevada, Las Vegas finds Artwalk to be a great showcase of our interdisciplinary status between the college of engineering and college of fine arts. The DOCK is a completely EED-student-run project consisting of multiple subprojects, including:

  • Audio
  • Lighting
  • LED
  • Content Creation
  • Graphic Design
  • Event Design
  • Schematics
  • Fabrications
  • Prop Making
  • Videography
  • Project Management
  • Hospitality / Marketing
  • Stagehand / Set-Up
  • Behind the Scenes
  • Web Development


Our Goal:

The Entertainment Engineering Program finds Artwalk to be a great showcase of our interdisciplinary status between the college of engineering and college of fine arts.
